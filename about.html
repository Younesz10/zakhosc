About Zakho SC - History & Mission

Zakho SC History

Zakho SC (Zakho Sport Club) was founded in 1987 in Zakho, Kurdistan Region, Iraq. The club has grown to become a symbol of pride for the city and its people, competing in the Iraq Stars League and representing the region with passion and dedication. Zakho SC has established itself as one of the most respected football clubs in Kurdistan and Iraq.

Mission & Values

  • Promote football and sportsmanship in Zakho and beyond
  • Represent Zakho with pride and integrity
  • Develop local talent and inspire the next generation
  • Foster unity and community spirit

Zakho International Stadium

Zakho International Stadium is the official home ground of Zakho SC, providing a modern venue for fans to support their team and enjoy the beautiful game. The stadium serves as the heart of football in Zakho and hosts all home matches for Zakho Sport Club in the Iraq Stars League.
