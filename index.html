Zakho SC - Premier Football Club in Kurdistan

Zakho SC (Zakho Sport Club) is a professional football team representing the city of Zakho in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq. Founded in 1987, Zakho SC competes in the Iraq Stars League and has become one of the most respected football clubs in the region.

Our home matches are played at the Zakho International Stadium, where passionate fans gather to support their team. The club's rich history, dedicated players, and loyal fanbase make Zakho SC a cornerstone of football culture in Kurdistan.
